Upcoming Events

  • September 16- Celebrity Doug's Ultimate Rib King Competition

    • The best of the best BBQ dads across Iowa compete to become the Rib King! Samples of our signature sauces will be provided to the public, and bottles will be provided to competitors if they wish. Sign-up will occur at 10:00 AM at the Jordan Creek Town Center in Des Moines, IA for those wishing to compete. Official event will begin at 12:00 PM.

  • September 17- Celebrity Doug's Ultimate Rib Queen Competition

    • Similar to our Celebrity Doug's Ultimate Rib King Competition, but for women. Compete head to head using Celebrity Doug's Famous BBQ to determine who the true Queen of BBQ. Sign-up will occur at 10:00 AM at the Jordan Creek Town Center in Des Moines, IA for those wishing to compete. Official event will begin at 12:00 PM.

  • Also September 17- Celebrity Doug's Ultimate Rib Civil War

    • The true BBQ test, the named King of BBQ will face-off against the Queen of BBQ in an all out BBQ war. Each competitor will make a three course BBQ meal. The winner will be crowned BBQ Overlord and win 32 bottles of Celebrity Doug's BBQ sauce. Official event will begin at 5:00 PM at the Jordan Creek Town Center in Des Moines, IA.

  • September 30- Hurricane Harvey & Irma Relief Dinner

    • Celebrity Doug's BBQ is a big believer in offering a helping hand to those in need. Please join us at our Hurricane Harvey & Irma Relief Dinner to help us offer monetary donations to all those affected by these disastrous storms. The event will take place at HyVee™ Hall in Des Moines, IA with a general admission of $15 for all you can eat BBQ. Children under 8 years of age eat free. Extra donations will also be accepted throughout the event.
