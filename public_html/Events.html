Upcoming Events

  • September 16- Celebrity Doug's Ultimate Rib King Competition

    • The best of the best BBQ dads across Iowa compete to become the Rib King! Samples of our signature sauces will be provided to the public, and bottles will be provided to competitors if they wish. Sign-up will occur at 10:00 AM at the Jordan Creek Town Center in Des Moines, IA for those wishing to compete. Official event will begin at 12:00 PM.

  • September 30- Hurricane Harvey & Irma Relief Dinner

    • Celebrity Doug's BBQ is a big believer in offering a helping hand to those in need. Please join us at our Hurricane Harvey & Irma Relief Dinner to help us offer monetary donations to all those affected by these disastrous storms. The event will take place at HyVee™ Hall in Des Moines, IA with a general admission of $15 for all you can eat BBQ. Children under 8 years of age eat free. Extra donations will also be accepted throughout the event.

  • October 31- Celebrity Doug's Halloween Extravaganza

    • Celebrity Doug's loves BBQ, but we also love to have a good time! On October 31, we will be having a Halloween Extravaganza on Court Ave in Des Moines, Iowa. Admission is $20 and includes all you can eat BBQ. There will also be inflatables for the kids and tailgating games for the adults. There will also be a costume contest with a $100 grand prize. The event starts at 11:00 AM and will run until 3:00 PM. We hope to see you there!

  • December 23- Celebrity Doug's Christmas Bash

    • Celebrity Doug's is throwing a Christmas Bash and you are invited! Make sure to come down to Celebrity Doug's restaurant in Indianola for some FREE BBQ. Yes! You read that right, FREE BBQ! There is one catch, it is only availible for the first 100 people at the restaurant. The event will start at 11:30 AM and will run until 3:30 PM. There will be samples and fun games to play! You don't want to miss it!

  • December 31- Celebrity Doug's New Year's Eve Party

    • Celebrity Doug's is hosting a New Year's Eve Party at their Indianola location. The event will start at 4:00 PM and will run until 2:00 AM. There will be door prizes and all you can eat BBQ. The cost for the event is $17.50, but the first 25 people are free! Make sure to come out early and stay late! The party never stops at Celebrity Doug's Famous BBQ!
